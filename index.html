Background

My Personal Background.

My name is Eduardo Henriques Kerr, 24 years old as of 2022I'm an independent Game Developer and Programmer. I'm currently base in in São Paulo, Brazil. When it comes to game development I can work in a wide variety of projects, and can adapt to new requirements if the client needs it. I studied Computer Science at UFSCar for two years and have been studying Game Design at Anhembi Morumbi with course completion in the end of 2022. I've studied games and game design / development, way before college, by myself, my main focus is the indie market, and although I don't have AAA environment experience, I open to this kind of oportunity.

I'm interested in working with variety of scenarios to keep learning new tech, make new connections and learning more about the industry. I love games and love how they affect people, my passion is to take my job seriously so the end product is the best version of itself that I can guarantee.

Even though I may be working at a project already I'm always interested in knowing of new projects and I'm open to participating, feel free to contact me by any of the means down on the "Contact" section!

Have a great day : ).
